FAQ

1

How do I take care of my plants afterwards?

We offer insights into watering schedules, lighting requirements, and overall maintenance. We provide the necessary knowledge and materials to ensure that every plant thrives, contributing to a greener and healthier indoor environment.

2

Can I choose the amount of plants?

Absolutely! Tailor the quantity of plants to suit your preferences and the available space. Whether opting for a minimalist touch with a few statement plants or creating a lush oasis with numerous varieties, the choice is yours to curate a personalized and inviting atmosphere.

3

How do you integrate plants into designs?

Integrating plants into designs involves thoughtful placement and selection. Incorporate them as focal points or use them to soften architectural lines. Consider plant types, sizes, and containers to complement the design aesthetic. Whether through hanging planters, potted greenery, or vertical gardens, plants add vitality and harmony to interiors, fostering a connection with nature.

4

How do you ensure plant longevity in designs?

Ensuring plant longevity in designs involves strategic planning. Consider factors like natural light, temperature, and maintenance needs. Choose plants suited to the environment and provide proper watering and care instructions. Incorporate self-watering systems if applicable. Regularly assess the health of the plants and replace or relocate them as needed to maintain a vibrant and enduring design.

5

What are the prices?

Prices for interior design services vary based on your specific budget and requirements. The scope of the project, the level of customization, and the choice of materials all contribute to the overall cost. A tailored design solution can be crafted to align with your budget while ensuring a personalized and satisfying outcome.
