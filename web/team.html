Meet the team

A bit

of our history

origins

Flora Fusion, an innovative interior design company, was born out of a deep love for nature and a vision to bring its tranquility into urban spaces.

The founders, a trio of design enthusiasts, Jose Clavijo, Manuela Olivero, and Ruan Mei were inspired by their shared childhood experiences in the countryside, surrounded by lush greenery and the serene sounds of nature.

They realized that while city life offered many conveniences, it often lacked the calming influence of nature. With this in mind, they set out to create a company that would fuse the beauty of flora with the functionality of interior design, hence the name Flora Fusion.

Current

Flora Fusion is a unique blend of Jose’s creative interior design, Manuela’s botanical expertise, and Ruan Mei’s efficient project management.

Together with the expert team, they seamlessly fuse the tranquility of nature with urban spaces, creating sustainable and aesthetically pleasing interiors.
